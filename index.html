Moving abroad for university or work is exciting — but finding the right accommodation can feel overwhelming. S‑Let helps international students and professionals search, secure and settle into safe private accommodation across UK & EU cities, with guidance at every stage.

Your Trusted Partner in Private Renting

S‑Let helps international students and professionals secure private accommodation with confidence. From property search to move in and beyond, we support you at every step—so you can focus on your studies or career while we handle the housing details across the UK and EU.

Accommodation Search Specialists

We search, shortlist, and recommend verified private accommodation based on your budget, location, and personal requirements. Every option is carefully selected to match your lifestyle and priorities.

Continuous Support While You Settle In

Unlike traditional agents, our support doesn’t stop once you sign. We stay with you throughout your tenancy—offering guidance, cultural insights, and practical help during and after move‑in.

Advocating for You

With trusted relationships with landlords and agents, we represent your interests clearly and ensure a fair renting experience.

Tenancy & Contract Support

We explain your rental contract in clear terms, highlight obligations, and ensure everything is understood before you commit.

Rent Guarantor Assistance

S‑Let is recognised by selected landlords and agents as a guarantor provider. We guide your eligibility and support applications when external providers are needed.

Additional Services (“The Boring Stuff”)

Utilities setup, council tax guidance, TV licensing, cleaning, furniture installation and removal, and end‑of‑tenancy help—so you can settle in without stress.
