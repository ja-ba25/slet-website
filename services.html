Our Services

Simple, Trusted Support for Renting Abroad

S‑Let is an independent accommodation search and support service created to help international students and their families navigate the private rental market with confidence.

We work exclusively for the renter — not landlords or agents — offering clear guidance, practical support, and ongoing assistance from the moment you start your search until the end of your tenancy.

Our services are designed for university‑age international students and their parents who want reassurance, clarity, and a reliable partner when renting in a new country.

Accommodation Search & Securing

We search for verified, available private accommodation based on your budget, location, and requirements. You receive carefully shortlisted options from trusted landlords and agents — saving time, stress, and uncertainty.

  • Personalised search
  • Virtual or in‑person viewings
  • Availability verification
  • Guidance on deposits, fees, and rental terms

Tenancy Guidance & Ongoing Support

We explain your tenancy agreement in clear, simple language and highlight what matters most before you commit. After move‑in, we remain available to support you throughout your tenancy.

  • Contract explanation
  • Liaison with landlords or agents
  • Support with issues, repairs, or misunderstandings
  • Independent guidance throughout your stay

Rent Guarantor Services

Many international renters are asked for a local guarantor. Where possible, S‑Let can offer limited guarantor support or help you prepare acceptable documentation. If an external guarantor is required, we connect you with trusted, accredited providers and guide you through the process.

Practical Support Services

We assist with the everyday tasks that come with renting abroad, helping students settle in smoothly.

  • Utilities setup guidance
  • Council tax advice (where applicable)
  • Cleaning services
  • Furniture installation or removal
  • End‑of‑tenancy support

As our service develops, we will introduce carefully selected partner services to support student wellbeing, legal guidance, and relocation needs.
