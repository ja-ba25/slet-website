Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be in the UK to search for accommodation?

No. We arrange virtual viewings and provide full remote support. You can secure accommodation before arriving in the UK or EU.

Can you help me understand my tenancy agreement?

Yes. We explain every clause in plain language, highlight key obligations, and make sure you fully understand the contract before signing.

Do I need a UK guarantor?

Not always. Some landlords accept upfront rent or alternatives. If a guarantor is required, we can connect you with trusted providers and guide you through the process.

What if I have an issue with my landlord?

We liaise with landlords or agents on your behalf to resolve repairs, disputes, or misunderstandings quickly and professionally.

Can parents speak to S‑Let directly?

Absolutely. Families often choose direct communication for reassurance, updates, and clear guidance throughout the tenancy.

Which cities and countries do you cover?

We cover major UK and EU university cities. If your destination is not listed, contact us and we will assess how we can help.
